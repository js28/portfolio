Civics Action Project (CAP Project)

April 2018-May 2018, 8th Grade History

The Civics Action Project (CAP project for short) was a group project where a group of people had to pick an issue with the world and brainstorm ways to fix it. My group and I thought it would be good for us to try and find/plan out a better healthcare system. Along with just creating the board and writing out information, taking polls and researching, we had to take larger steps to try and better our country's healthcare. We contacted Congressman Swalwell, we devised a new Healthcare Plan that we think would better suit our country's needs.
